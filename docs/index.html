About Us

Tilipamodzi Engineering Group is a multidisciplinary engineering and construction company based in Malawi, providing reliable, affordable, and sustainable solutions. We specialize in engineering design, construction services, technical support, and digital solutions.

Our mission is to deliver professional engineering services that contribute to Malawi's infrastructure development while maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and sustainability.
